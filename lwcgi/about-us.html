About us

Loveworld Church Growth International is a strategic vehicle of the Loveworld Ministry set up to drive the complete attainment of the overall goals of the Ministry in reaching the entire world with our unique message of the gospel.

With a suite of tools, analytics, and learning programmes based on the messages of our Man of God, deployed both tactically and as remedial intervention to bridge capacity gaps, Loveworld Church Growth International presents the platform for all stakeholders (leaders and members) in the Ministry to be adequately equipped to fulfill their inidividual roles in accomplishment of our Ministry mandate.

Why LWCGI

  • Our Vision

    The Vision of the Loveworld Church Growth International is to promote sustained spiritual and numerical growth in our churches, commensurate with the expectations of the Ministry and the urgency of our Kingdom Agenda.

  • Our Mission

    Loveworld Church Growth International will work with nominated Churches to deploy specific, strategic and tactical intervention for sustained growth; through focused and intense engagement with the Church leadership and brethren, by a select Team of Pastors equipped to produce the desired outcome.

  • Our Goal

    Create opportunities of abroad higher study and improve significantly family & position and national development and to contribute to achieve Bangladesh as a middle income country.
